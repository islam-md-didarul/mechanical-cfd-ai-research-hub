# AI for CFD

## Prerequisites

Complete the Python/mathematics and CFD-foundations paths first.

## Recommended order

1. [jonkrohn/ML-foundations](https://github.com/jonkrohn/ML-foundations)
2. [shaiful019/MLOB1](https://github.com/shaiful019/MLOB1)
3. [AndreWeiner/ml-cfd-lecture](https://github.com/AndreWeiner/ml-cfd-lecture)
4. [WillDreamer/Awesome-AI4CFD](https://github.com/WillDreamer/Awesome-AI4CFD)
5. [lukasmasuch/best-of-ml-python](https://github.com/lukasmasuch/best-of-ml-python)

## Application sequence

1. Define the physical and research question.
2. Generate or collect validated CFD/experimental data.
3. Build train/validation/test splits that prevent leakage.
4. Establish a physics-based or classical reduced-order baseline.
5. Train the data-driven model.
6. Evaluate accuracy, generalization, conservation, and robustness.
7. Compare computational cost with the original solver.

# CFD Foundations

## Objective

Connect conservation equations to working numerical algorithms and understand the concepts behind commercial CFD tools.

## Recommended order

1. [islam-md-didarul/CFDPython](https://github.com/islam-md-didarul/CFDPython)
2. [islam-md-didarul/Python_CFD](https://github.com/islam-md-didarul/Python_CFD)
3. [islam-md-didarul/CFD-Python](https://github.com/islam-md-didarul/CFD-Python)
4. [islam-md-didarul/staggered-grid-lid-driven-cavity](https://github.com/islam-md-didarul/staggered-grid-lid-driven-cavity)
5. [islam-md-didarul/PyCFD](https://github.com/islam-md-didarul/PyCFD)

## Expected outcomes

- Explain conservation of mass and momentum.
- Understand spatial and temporal discretization.
- Recognize stability, convergence, conservation, and boundary-condition errors.
- Implement elementary structured-grid cases.
- Understand the organization of a research solver.

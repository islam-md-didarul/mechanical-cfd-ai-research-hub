# Solid Mechanics and Fluid–Structure Interaction

## Recommended path

1. Mathematics and Python foundations
2. [felupe](https://github.com/islam-md-didarul/felupe)
3. CFD foundations
4. Separate validation of the fluid and structural models
5. One-way coupling
6. Two-way coupling
7. Sensitivity to time step, coupling iterations, mesh motion, and material properties

## Potential applications

- Flexible airway-wall modeling
- Pump or blade deformation
- Pressure-induced structural loading
- Hyperelastic components
- Fluid-driven vibration

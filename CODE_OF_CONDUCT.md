# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free
environment for everyone who participates in the Mechanical CFD & Scientific AI
Research Hub, regardless of experience level, background, identity, discipline,
institution, or location.

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and constructively;
- provide technically grounded feedback;
- acknowledge sources, licenses, and prior work;
- distinguish verified facts from personal interpretation;
- welcome questions from students and new contributors;
- focus criticism on ideas, methods, data, and documentation rather than people;
- respect privacy and avoid sharing confidential or personally identifying data.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable conduct includes:

- harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or personal attacks;
- insulting or dismissive language;
- deliberate misinformation or fabricated references;
- plagiarism or removal of required attribution;
- publishing private information without permission;
- repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or reviews;
- submitting malicious links, files, scripts, or dependencies.

## Scope

This code applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews,
commits, and other project-related communication.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer through an
appropriate private contact method. Do not include sensitive personal
information in a public issue.

The maintainer may edit or remove content, close discussions, reject
contributions, issue warnings, or temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when necessary to protect the community.

## Attribution

This project code of conduct is informed by common open-source community
standards and has been adapted for an engineering research and education
repository.
